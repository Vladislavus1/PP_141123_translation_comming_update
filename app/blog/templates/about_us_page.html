YourArticlesHub

Is the place where knowledge, ideas and passion for discussion come together. Create, share, discuss and get inspired with us.

Main functions:

1.Creation and publication of articles:
Registered users can create and publish articles on free topics. Thanks to the intuitive interface and article editor,
the process of creating content becomes easy and enjoyable.

2.Review of articles:
Users can browse through articles sorted by various categories and keywords to find the ones that interest them.

3.Likes and comments:
The ability to put likes allows you to express approval or interest in the article. Also, users can leave comments under the articles,
initiating discussion and interaction.

4.User profiles:
Each registered user has his own unique profile where he can display his publications, received likes, comments and other activity.

5.Recommendation settings:
Users can customize their recommendations by choosing topics and keywords of interest.
This allows you to personalize your news feed and review articles.

6.Community and Engagement:
YourArticlesHub
promotes a community of like-minded people by providing a platform for communication,
exchange of opinions and scientific knowledge.

7.Moderation and security:
Moderation mechanisms and feedback systems are used to maintain the quality of content and prevent objectionable material.
